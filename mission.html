My Mission

Quite a few times in today’s society, we the people, sometimes forget to appreciate other people for their good deeds benefitting the society. I want to make a website showcasing these people’s contributions. According to me, if someone doesn’t get the credit they deserve, then they might get demotivated to do more. On the contrary, if someone is appreciated by others, then they will get inspired to do even greater things. Especially during this Pandemic, many frontline workers and other essential people were unappreciated for the crucial work they did, risking their own lives in the process. It is very important to reach my target audience because it will be fundamental for themselves as they might get motivated to do great things after reading about the great things their peers did.
